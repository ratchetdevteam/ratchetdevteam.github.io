Ratchet Games was established in early 2012 by a group of entrepreneurs inspired to make unique and challenging games. Ratchet is currently developing CRAG, a third-person multiplayer online battle arena game that aims for a fresh and diverse look on an industry standard.

Armand Sorko-Ram Game Designer & Game Director

  • management
  • communication
  • rigging
  • animation
  • programming

Armand, from Tracy, California, grew up in the late MS-DOS arcade age of gaming. He was astounded by the amazing innovations from the golden age of Nintendo, Sega, and Sony. After high school he started a successful gaming review website and YouTube channel called BossVG. Later, when the group disbanded, he attended Ex’pression College for Digital Arts to learn how to create what he passionately cared about - game design. There, he selected students to gather and created Team CoS, who created Into the Waste and Fission, each developed within an eight week timeline. Since he took programming and game design roles in each project, he decided to focus on the technical aspects of gaming and formed an even stronger team - Ratchet Games.
